1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Quick Download TV ("the App", "we", 
"us", or "our") handles user information. Quick Download TV is a 
professional file management tool designed for Fire TV and Android TV 
devices. By using our application, you agree to the practices described 
in this Privacy Policy.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Quick Download TV does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal 
information to external servers. We are committed to user privacy and 
have designed our application to operate entirely on your device.

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data or GPS coordinates
- Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, advertising ID)
- Browsing history or visited URLs
- Download history sent to external servers
- Usage analytics or telemetry data
- Crash reports
- Any other personal or sensitive information

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

3. LOCAL DATA STORAGE

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never 
transmitted to any external server:

- Application preferences and settings (cursor speed, configuration)
- User-added favorites and bookmarks
- Local download history
- WebView cookies (required for proper website functionality)
- Downloaded files in the device storage

This data remains under your complete control and is automatically 
removed when you uninstall the application.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4. PERMISSIONS REQUIRED

Quick Download TV requests the following Android permissions, each 
with a specific purpose:

INTERNET
Purpose: Required to download files from URLs you specify
Justification: Core functionality of the download manager

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
Purpose: To verify internet connectivity before downloads
Justification: Prevents failed downloads due to network issues

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Purpose: To save downloaded files to device storage
Justification: Essential for file management functionality

REQUEST_INSTALL_PACKAGES
Purpose: To install Android applications (APK files) you download
Justification: User-initiated installation of personal files

FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Purpose: To continue downloads in the background
Justification: Improved user experience and download reliability

WAKE_LOCK
Purpose: To prevent device sleep during active downloads
Justification: Ensures download completion without interruption

ACCESS_WIFI_STATE
Purpose: To detect WiFi connection status
Justification: Better network management for downloads
